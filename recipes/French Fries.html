French Fries

Description

This French fries recipe is made using a clever, proven cooking method that guarantees crispy fries - and they STAY crispy for ages!

  • 500 grams potato
  • 2 cups of oil
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • paprika
  1. Cut the potato
  2. Wash it
  3. Season it
  4. put oil in a pan
  5. fry it at high heat for 10 mins
  6. add some ketchup at the end
